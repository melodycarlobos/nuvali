Biking

NUVALI will have off-road biking and running trails approximately 50km in length which offers scenic views of the NUVALI terrain for running and biking. At certain areas, one can enjoy sights of Mount Makiling, Laguna de Bay and the Tagaytay Ridge. Four-cross and pump bike tracks are also open to bike enthusiasts who are interested to practice and hold events.

The trails are open to the public. Interested parties may proceed to the Evoliving Center concierge to register. A valid ID is required.

Greens & Patches

A 3-hectare tree nursery and demo farm provides families, students and individuals a venue where they can learn while they enjoy fun activities outdoors.

Visitors can learn about the different tree and plant species and its benefits. One can also enjoy vegetable and fruit picking among other activities.

Activities include:
  • Fishing
  • Vegetable picking
  • Organic farm lecturing
Amenities include:
  • Nipa huts for dining
  • Thematic gardens (spice garden, herbal garden etc)
  • Tree Nursery
  • Kitchen
  • Dining area (Nipa huts for rent)
  • Fishing pond
